Takeshi Obata

One of Japan's great manga artist

“There are no nudists in cold places” - TO


Here's some of Takeshi's famous works :



Hikaru No Go The story is based on the game Go, a board game popular in Japan. It's a character driven story told from the point of view of a 12 year old kid name Hikaru.




Bakuman The story follows talented artist Moritaka Mashiro and aspiring writer Akito Takagi, two ninth grade boys who wish to become manga artists




Death Note The story follows Light Yagami, a teen genius who stumbles across a mysterious otherworldly notebook: the "Death Note", which belonged to the Shinigami Ryuk.
